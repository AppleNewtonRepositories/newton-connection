Restoring information

If something goes wrong and you lose information on your Newton device, you can restore information from the latest work ﬁle on your computer.

You can restore all the information in a work ﬁle or select which information you want to restore.

WARNING!

Restoring replaces information on your Newton device with information from a work ﬁle on your computer. Make sure you select the correct information to restore.

To restore information:

Click the Restore button on the Newton Connection window.

Select the information you want to restore to your Newton device and click Restore.

Acknowledge the warning about the consequences of a restore and click OK.

The information you selected is restored to your Newton device. The status box in the Newton Connection window keeps you informed of progress.

When you restore, only information and packages on your Newton device that have corresponding information and packages in the backup ﬁle are replaced. For example, if your work ﬁle contains Names and Dates and your Newton device contains Names, Dates, and Notes, the Names and Dates on your Newton device are replaced by Names and Dates from the work ﬁle, but Notes remains intact.

When the restoration is complete, a message appears on your Newton screen asking you to restart.

Tap Restart.

If you have a personal identiﬁcation number (PIN) assigned to your Newton device, the number is not restored. You’ll need to reassign a PIN number. For more information on assigning a PIN number, see the handbook that came with your Newton device.

You can restore information from a card’s backup ﬁle to a card. You cannot restore information from a card’s backup ﬁle to your Newton’s internal memory.
