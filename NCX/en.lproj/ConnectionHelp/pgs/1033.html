Copying text from a document

During a keyboard session, you can copy text from documents open on your computer to your Newton device.

To copy text from a document:

Set the insertion point on your Newton device.

On your computer, select some text and drag it onto the Newton Connection window, or paste text from the clipboard.

The text appears on your Newton device.

Text is copied, but graphics are not.

It may take a few seconds for large amounts to text to appear on your Newton device.
