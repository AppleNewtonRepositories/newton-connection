Controlling from your Newton device

The utilities may be accessed from the connection slip on your Newton device.

You can also set up connection preferences on your Newton device.

To change connection preferences:

Launch the Dock (or Connection) app in Extras.

Tap the information (i) button and choose Prefs from the list of choices.

Select the desired connection preferences.

Tap the close box (x) to close the slip.

You cannot change connection preferences while a connection is established between your Newton PDA and your computer.
