Ending a keyboard session

When you’ve ﬁnished entering information from the keyboard, you can end a keyboard session by any of the following methods.

To end a keyboard session:

On your computer, click the Device item in the Newton Connection window sidebar to return to the Device view.

On your Newton device, tap the Dock button in the Extras drawer.

Tap the blinking star (*) at the top of your Newton screen and tap Dock. When a message appears asking whether you want to end your keyboard session, tap Yes.
