Connecting via serial

To use a serial connection you need a serial cable to connect your Newton device to a serial adapter such as the Keyspan USA-28X, which is in turn connected to a USB port on your computer. Follow the instructions that came with your USB to serial adapter to install any necessary software on your computer.
