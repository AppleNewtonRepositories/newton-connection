Auto docking

The MessagePad 2000 and 2100 can detect when a Newton Serial Adapter is inserted into the InterConnect port, and automatically initiate a connection.

To auto dock:

On your Macintosh computer, launch Newton Connection and in the Device view of the window adjust the Auto Dock settings to back up or synchronize.

Plug the Serial Adapter into the InterConnect port on your Newton device.

Tap Auto Dock in the slip that appears on the Newton display.

A full backup or synchronization begins automatically.

If you use a connection password, you must set the Dock preferences to ‘Save password’.
