Compatibility

Newton Connection 2.0 connects to both Newton OS 1 and OS 2 devices.

When connecting to a Newton OS 1 device, select the function you require from the Newton 1 menu before establishing a connection.

That function will be performed once a connection is established, and the Newton device will be disconnected automatically once it has completed.
